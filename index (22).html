About Forrest

Meet the Boy Behind the Big Challenge 👋

Forrest is seven years old and in Class 2A at Sele First School in Hexham. He's the kind of kid who loves an adventure — whether that's exploring the countryside, running around the playground, or coming up with ambitious ideas. This summer, he's had his biggest idea yet.

Every child in his class was asked to choose their own fundraising challenge for the holidays. Forrest didn't want to do something easy. He wanted to do something that would make people sit up and think, "Wow, that's impressive!" So he chose to walk 150 miles across the six-week summer break.

That's roughly 3.5 miles every single day — including while on a family holiday in Venice! He hopes that the sheer effort involved will inspire people to sponsor him, helping to raise money for something that will benefit every child at his school.
