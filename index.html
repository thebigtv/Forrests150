Meet Forrest

The Boy Behind the Big Idea

Forrest is seven years old and has just finished Year 2 at Sele First School in Hexham. He's curious, determined, and - when he sets his mind to something - unstoppable.

When his class was asked to choose their own summer fundraising challenge, Forrest didn't want to pick something easy. He wanted to do something that would make people sit up and think, "That's genuinely impressive."

"I want to walk 150 miles because it's really hard - and that's the point. If it was easy, it wouldn't be worth sponsoring."

That means roughly 3.5 miles every single day for six weeks — including while his family are on holiday in Venice. Whatever the weather, whatever the day, Forrest keeps walking.
